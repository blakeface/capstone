Jerk

noun | /j…ôrk/

A change in acceleration. Also known as a jolt, surge, or lurch.

The Jerk Project measures cycling jerks.

It has no affiliation with Steve Martin films, Jamaican chicken, or unsavory people...

Act one: JerkLess

Jerkless is a hybrid iOS app that taps into the accelerometer and geolocation data of any iOS device.

Act two: JerkMaps

Jerkmaps is the visual results of the Jerkless data. Each point is color coded:

Green = jerkless

Red = jerky
